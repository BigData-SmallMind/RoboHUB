FULLY INTEGRATED Z-WAVE SOLUTIONS

Z-Wave is a wireless technology designed specifically for home automation, it is the best alternative to Zigbee, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth

It has a standardized wireless protocol so that devices from various manufacturers can communicate with one another

It uses mesh networking, making it long range and self-healing, that is if one device fails it wont have an effect on the whole network

There is an additional layer of security with encryption

It is very energy effecient and has a very long battery life
